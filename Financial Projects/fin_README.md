# Financial Projects

A collection of financial analyses exploring investment performance, competitive market positioning, and cost-benefit modeling using Excel and Tableau.

---

## Projects

### Coke vs. Pepsi Investment Analysis
Analyzed historical financial data for Coca-Cola and PepsiCo to compare long-term investment performance. Supplemented with a Tableau analysis of the current carbonated beverage market to contextualize each company's competitive position.

`Excel` `Tableau` `Financial Analysis`

---

### Index Investment Analysis
Compared the performance of various investment types at the turn of the century against their more recent performance using index data. Highlights how different asset classes have shifted in relative value over time.

`Excel` `Index Data` `Investment Analysis`

---

### Benefit-Cost Analysis of Trees
Research project evaluating the economic costs and benefits of urban tree planting, including determining the optimal harvest age for timber yield. Combines environmental economics with quantitative cost-benefit methodology.

`Excel` `Cost-Benefit Analysis` `Research`
